For a data professional, understanding the daily workflow, project ownership, and success metrics is critical. Asking these questions shows that you are results oriented and proactive about aligning your skills with the company&#39;s goals from day one.</p><p>These questions shift the conversation from you being a candidate to a potential collaborator. Are you an order taker who pulls data, or a strategic partner who uncovers insights? You need to know if success is measured by the number of dashboards built, the accuracy of your models, or the business impact of your findings.</p><blockquote><p><strong>Key Insight:</strong> Understanding how performance is measured is non negotiable.
