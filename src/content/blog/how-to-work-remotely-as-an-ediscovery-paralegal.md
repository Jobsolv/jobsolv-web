They help lawyers by getting and organizing digital documents like emails, text messages, and files.</p><p id="">Their job is to make sure attorneys have all the digital evidence they need for trials and hearings. This includes finding information, checking backgrounds, preparing legal papers for lawyer review, and helping with trial preparations.</p><p id="">These professionals need to know how to use various types of computer software that find and manage this electronic info.</p><p id="">They must be ready to work at odd hours because their tasks might align with different time zones. These leaders know how to give tasks well, even from afar.</p><p id="">They also understand each paralegal's needs for managing their workload effectively while ensuring billable hours are met.
